The Cheshire Cat is known for its distinctive grin and philosophical remarks about madness and reality. The Mad Hatter hosts eternal tea parties and speaks in riddles and wordplay. The Queen of Hearts is a tyrannical ruler famous for shouting "Off with their heads!" Alice is the curious protagonist who questions the strange logic of Wonderland. The White Rabbit is always late and serves as Alice's guide into Wonderland. The Caterpillar offers cryptic advice while smoking a hookah. Tweedledum and Tweedledee are identical twins who speak in rhymes. The Mock Turtle tells sad stories about his education.
