Famous quotes from Alice in Wonderland:

"We're all mad here" - Cheshire Cat
This philosophical observation suggests that madness might be a normal state in Wonderland, or perhaps in life itself.

"Why is a raven like a writing desk?" - Mad Hatter
The famous unsolvable riddle that represents the playful nonsense and logical puzzles throughout the story.

"Off with their heads!" - Queen of Hearts  
The Queen's favorite exclamation shows her tyrannical nature and the arbitrary use of power.

"Curiouser and curiouser!" - Alice
Alice's expression of wonder as things become increasingly strange, showing her adaptability to new situations.

"I can't go back to yesterday because I was a different person then" - Alice
A profound reflection on personal growth and the impossibility of returning to who we once were.

"Everything's got a moral, if only you can find it" - Duchess
Suggests that meaning can be found in even the most absurd situations.
